## 🔧 What I Do

Currently, I design and implement vision solutions for real-world robotic systems, such as:

- **Bin picking** of cluttered objects (e.g., black bottles with occlusion)
- **Precise placement** using pose refinement (≤1–2 cm accuracy)
- **Vision-guided assembly systems** in industrial environments

My typical pipeline includes:

> Detection → 3D perception → Pose estimation → Grasp planning → Refinement → Execution
