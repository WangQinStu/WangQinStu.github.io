About

  • 3D perception with RGB-D cameras (RealSense D435i / D405)
  • 6D pose estimation (e.g., FoundationPose / openPose)
  • Robot grasping and manipulation
  • Camera calibration and hand-eye calibration
  • Point cloud processing (Open3D / PCL)

🔧 What I Do

Currently, I design and implement vision solutions for real-world robotic systems, such as:

  • Bin picking of cluttered objects (e.g., black bottles with occlusion)
  • Precise placement using pose refinement (≤1–2 cm accuracy)
  • Vision-guided assembly systems in industrial environments

My typical pipeline includes:

Detection → 3D perception → Pose estimation → Grasp planning → Refinement → Execution

🚀 Interests

I am particularly interested in the intersection of:

  • Embodied AI
  • Robotics + Machine Learning
  • 3D Vision and Scene Understanding
  • Efficient perception under constraints (latency, power, robustness)

I am currently exploring how learning-based methods (e.g., Transformers, RL, imitation learning) can be integrated into real-world industrial robotic systems.
